# 📝 Student Guide - Honor Your Life Poster

## Welcome!

This guide will help you create your own Honor Your Life poster. It's easy, and you don't need any coding experience!

## How to Get Started

1. **Open the Website**: Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Click "Create Poster"** from the main page
3. **Fill Out the Form** with your information (see below)
4. **Click "Create My Poster"** to generate your poster
5. **View Your Poster** in the gallery

## What Information to Include

### Personal Information
- **Full Name**: Your name
- **Indian Name** (optional): If you have an Indian name, include it here with the translation
- **Tribal Affiliation** (optional): If you're enrolled, which tribe?
- **Area You Live**: What area or community do you live in?
- **Photo**: Upload a link to your photo or family picture (or leave blank)

### About You
- **Likes & Hobbies**: What do you enjoy doing? What are your hobbies and interests?
- **Goals**: What are your short-term and long-term goals? What do you want to achieve?
- **Motto/Favorite Quote**: What inspires you? Share a favorite quote or motto
- **Your Strengths**: What strengths have your classmates or colleagues noticed in you? (Remember: In Blackfeet culture, praise is a high honor!)

## How to Add a Photo

1. Find a photo you want to use (could be yours or a family photo)
2. Upload it to a free site like:
   - Google Photos
   - Imgur
   - PhotoBucket
3. Get the **direct link** to the image
4. Paste that link in the "Photo URL" field

**Example link**: `https://example.com/myphoto.jpg`

## Viewing Your Poster

Once you create your poster:
- View it on screen
- Download it as an HTML file (you can open it anytime!)
- Edit the HTML code if you want to customize it further
- Print it out or share the HTML file with friends

## Editing Your Poster

If you want to make changes:

1. **From the Gallery**: Click your poster
2. **Click "Edit HTML"**
3. **Modify the code** to customize colors, fonts, layout, etc.
4. **Click "Save Changes"** to keep your edits

### Basic HTML You Can Edit

```html
<h2 style="color: red;">This is red text</h2>
<p style="font-size: 20px;">This text is bigger</p>
<p style="background: yellow;">Yellow background</p>
```

## Tips for a Great Poster

✨ **Be Authentic**: Share real information about yourself
💭 **Be Thoughtful**: Take time to reflect on your goals and strengths
🎨 **Be Creative**: Use the HTML editor to customize colors and styling
👥 **Be Respectful**: When sharing your strengths, be honest about what others have told you
🌟 **Be Proud**: This is your Honor Your Life poster - wear it with pride!

## Sharing Your Poster

You can:
- **Download** it and share the HTML file with friends
- **Print** it out to display
- **Screenshot** it to share on social media
- **View others'** posters in the gallery for inspiration
